Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Any page size, any dimensions

Set your document to any width and height you need. Values are applied directly to the document via the Google Docs API — no workarounds, no hacks.

Inches, centimeters, or points

Switch units at any time — your current values convert automatically. Your preferred unit is saved and restored every time you open the sidebar.

Standard size presets

Includes Letter, Legal, Tabloid, Executive, Folio, A3, A4, A5, B4, and B5 out of the box. You can also save your own custom presets by name for quick reuse.

Portrait & landscape

Switching orientation swaps your dimensions and correctly transposes your margins — so your left/right and top/bottom margins rotate with the page as they should.

Full margin control

Set all four margins independently, link them together so they all move as one, swap left and right for binding layouts, or apply printer-safe minimums (0.25 in) in one click.

Reset with a preview

Before resetting, a comparison table shows your current settings against the defaults side by side — so you know exactly what's going to change before you confirm.

Full-featured color picker

An HSL gradient canvas with a hue slider, a smart hex input with autocomplete, and a palette of 80+ preset colors. Type a shorthand like #f0f and it expands automatically.

Saved custom colors

Save up to 30 of your own colors for quick reuse. They persist across sessions and right-clicking any saved swatch lets you remove it.

Live aspect ratio preview

A small page silhouette updates in real time as you type dimensions, giving you an instant visual sense of your page's proportions before applying anything.
