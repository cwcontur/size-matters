Privacy policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026  ·  Size Matters for Google Docs

Your privacy is important. This policy explains what information Size Matters ("the Add-on") collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information we collect

Size Matters does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information on external servers. The Add-on operates entirely within your browser session and Google Docs environment.

The only data stored is:

  • Custom color cache: Any page colors you save are stored locally using Google Apps Script's PropertiesService, scoped to your individual Google account. This data never leaves Google's infrastructure and is not accessible to the developer.

2. How we use your information

Because no personal data is collected, there is nothing to use, sell, share, or analyze. The Add-on applies page size and formatting changes directly to your open document at your direction and does nothing else.

3. Third-party services

Size Matters does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third party.

4. Google API scopes

The Add-on requests only the minimum Google API permissions necessary to function:

  • documents.currentonly — grants access to the currently open document only. Size Matters uses this exclusively to read and write page size, margin, and background color settings. It cannot access any other documents in your Google Drive.
  • script.container.ui — allows the Add-on to display its sidebar panel inside Google Docs.

Importantly, Size Matters cannot see, read, or access the text content of your document in any way. The Add-on only interacts with document-level formatting properties — specifically page dimensions, margins, and page color. Your writing stays completely private.

5. Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the Add-on after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at sizematterssupport@gmail.com.
